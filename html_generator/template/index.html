The supplemental materials are organized as follow:

  • Click on any Sketch ID to open that sketch's page.
  • Sketch pages present all the information about a sketch, its variants (thresholded, vectorized), and its professional and algorithmic cleanings.
  • The pages have toggle buttons to show and hide information, table headers that allow filtering and sorting, and a dropdown menu to choose the metric.
  • The displayed charts, when present, reflect the information shown in the table, taking into acount filtering and searching.
